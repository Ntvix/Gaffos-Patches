Gaffos Patches

Gaffo’s Patches is your go-to source for high-quality Irish emergency service patches. Passionate about all things frontline, Gaffo is especially fond of the Garda I40 Saloon—a standout favorite in his collection. Offering a wide range of custom and replica patches for units like POU, DDU, RPU, NAS, DFB, and more, Gaffo’s Patches covers every branch of the Irish emergency services. But it doesn’t stop there—international patches and services like Dublin Bus are also being added soon, expanding the collection beyond borders. Whether you're a collector, roleplayer, or enthusiast, Gaffo’s got a patch for you.
